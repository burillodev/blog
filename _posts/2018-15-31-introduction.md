Congress conveys its general vision by enacting laws; regulatory bodies, like the CFPB, implement that vision by publishing detailed regulations. These regulations are usually long, monolithic, difficult-to-read documents buried deep within Federal agency websites. With the recently released [eRegulations project](http://www.consumerfinance.gov/eregulations) ([source code](http://eregs.github.io/eregulations)), we aimed to make regulations more approachable by presenting them with structure and layers of relevant material
